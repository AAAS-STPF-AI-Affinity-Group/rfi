I. Introduction
SCORE, a nonprofit organization dedicated to mentoring and supporting small businesses,
recognizes the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in driving economicgrowth and business success. AI offers small businesses an unprecedented opportunity toenhance efficiency, reduce costs, and compete effectively in the marketplace. This proposaloutlines how SCORE can leverage AI to support small businesses and recommends policiesthat the federal government can adopt to facilitate AI integration at the small business level.
II. The Role of AI in Small Business Growth
AI can provide small businesses with solutions in multiple areas, including:
Customer Relationship Management (CRM): AI-powered chatbots and automationtools can streamline customer service and engagement.
Marketing and Sales Optimization : AI-driven analytics help small businesses
understand consumer behavior and tailor marketing strategies.
Financial Management : Automated bookkeeping and AI-powered forecasting tools
can enhance decision-making.
Operational Efficiency : AI-enabled inventory management and supply chain
optimization reduce costs and inefficiencies.
III. SCORE’s AI Action Plan for Small Businesses
SCORE proposes a structured AI action plan to support small businesses through education,mentorship, and accessible AI solutions. The key components of this plan include:
1. AI Training and Awareness Programs
Develop educational materials and workshops on AI applications for smallbusinesses.
Partner with AI experts to provide webinars, training sessions, and mentorship.


Create an online AI knowledge hub with case studies, best practices, and tutorials.
2. AI Adoption Mentorship Initiative
Pair small businesses with AI-savvy SCORE mentors who can guide them
through AI adoption.
Establish an AI Help Desk to provide personalized recommendations on AI toolsand vendors.
3. AI-Enabled Business Diagnostics
Implement AI-powered assessments to evaluate business needs and recommendAI solutions.
Develop tools that help small businesses analyze their operations and identify AIopportunities.
4. Collaborations with AI Innovators and Government Agencies
Partner with technology firms and universities to offer AI solutions tailored forsmall businesses.
Work with federal agencies to secure AI funding and grants for small businessadoption.
Advocate for policies that encourage AI-driven small business growth and protectentrepreneurs from AI-related risks.
IV. Federal Policy Recommendations
To maximize AI’s impact on small businesses, SCORE recommends the followinggovernment actions:
1. Funding and Grants:
 Establish AI adoption grants and low-interest loans for small
businesses investing in AI.
2. AI Literacy Programs:  Support national AI training initiatives to enhance digital skills
among small business owners.
3. Ethical AI Standards:  Ensure that AI applications used by small businesses adhere to
ethical guidelines and prevent data exploitation.
4. Public-Private Partnerships:  Foster collaboration between AI developers, government
agencies, and small business organizations to create user-friendly AI solutions.
V. Conclusion
AI has the potential to revolutionize small business operations, making them more competitiveand resilient. By implementing this AI Action Plan, SCORE aims to equip small businesseswith the knowledge, tools, and support they need to successfully integrate AI into their


operations. We urge the federal government to support these initiatives through strategic
investments and policy frameworks that ensure inclusive and responsible AI adoption. Perhaps
most important is the fact that SCORE is a volunteer organization, so implementation will beat no additional cost to taxpayers.
SCORE is committed to working alongside policymakers, technology leaders, and small
business owners to build an AI-driven future that benefits all American entrepreneurs.
Robert SerabinChair of SCORE-NYC AI Task Force
