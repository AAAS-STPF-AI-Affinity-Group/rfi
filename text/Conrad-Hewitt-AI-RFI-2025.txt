1. Prioritizing AI Research & Development (R&D)
-Increase federal funding for AI research in both academia and industry.
-Encourage public-private partnerships to accelerate breakthroughs in AI applications.
-Establish AI research hubs across the U.S. to decentralize innovation.
2. Workforce Development & Education
-Invest in AI education programs from K-12 to university levels.-Provide workforce retraining initiatives to help workers transition into AI-driven industries.-Create incentives for companies to offer AI-related apprenticeships and certifications.
3. Ethical AI & Responsible Innovation
-Develop clear but flexible guidelines for AI ethics to prevent misuse while allowing
innovation.
-Require transparency in AI decision-making, especially in high-stakes areas like healthcare
and law enforcement.
-
Support AI safety research to prevent unintended consequences and biases.
4. AI & National Security-Ensure the U.S. remains at the forefront of AI advancements in defense and cybersecurity.-Strengthen safeguards against AI threats, including misinformation and cyberattacks.-
Foster international collaboration with allies to establish global AI standards.
5. Regulatory Balance & Private Sector Growth-
Avoid overly burdensome regulations that could stifle AI startups and small businesses.


-Promote open AI ecosystems that encourage competition rather than monopolization.
-Streamline AI patent and intellectual property protections to incentivize innovation.
